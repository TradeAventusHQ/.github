# TradeAventus

**Where India and Europe Mean Business.**

[www.TradeAventus.com](https://www.tradeaventus.com)

TradeAventus is a Munich-based B2B trade platform connecting verified Indian suppliers with European buyers across six priority industrial sector clusters. Founded in 2025, the platform is positioned around the coming EU-India Free Trade Agreement and replaces paid visibility with earned verification tiers.

## Sector clusters

- Machinery, Industrial Parts & Automation
- - Automotive, Aerospace & Transportation
  - - Pharmaceuticals, Healthcare & Medical Devices
    - - Textiles, Apparel & Leather Goods
      - - Chemicals, Petrochemicals & Plastics
        - - Metals, Minerals & Advanced Materials
         
          - ## Why TradeAventus
         
          - - **Trust-first.** Verification tiers, not ad slots. Suppliers earn visibility through verification, not payment for placement.
            - - **Bi-directional corridor.** Indian suppliers to European buyers, and European suppliers to Indian buyers.
              - - **Built for the corridor's moment.** The EU-India Free Trade Agreement was concluded in January 2026 and is in ratification; the EU Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism has been live since 1 January 2026.
